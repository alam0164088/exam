How It Works

Using our nasal clip is effortless and effective. Follow these steps to enjoy better airflow and relief from snoring or congestion.

Step 1

Clean


Clean your nose thoroughly to remove any dirt or oil from the skin. Let your skin dry completely before removing the Tabs.
Pro-Tip: Cleanse with soap and water or use an alcohol prep pad for best results.

Step 2

Apply

Using the Applicator, apply one adhesive Tab to each side of your nose, just above the nostrils. Press each Tab onto your skin to ensure complete contact. Wait a few minutes before attaching the Band.

Step 3

Tab Placement

There are some tips to use I-Will. Place the plastic disc directly above the nostril (not on the nostril). Combined incident fits around the nostril and cheek. Always be sure to adjust Tabs properly. If the Tabs aren’t placed well, the Band will not attach well.

Step 4

Attach

Place the I-Will Band over your nose. The magnetic ends of the Band will snap onto the Tabs.
Pro-Tip: Make sure the Band is not upside down. The magnets should be facing down.
